Bean & Cheese Tacos Recipe

This is a bean and cheese taco. One of the best inventions made my humans. You can pair it with anything you can imagine. Here's a tip from me: Mix the bean and cheese with some cooked potatos and bacon and now you have the ultimate bean and cheese taco. Trust.

Ingredients

  • HEB Tortillas
  • Red Kidney Beans
  • Pork Skin
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Shredded Cheese
  • (Optional) Diced Potatos
  • (Optional) Bacon

Steps

  1. Add kidney beans, pork skin, water, and salt to a slow cooking pot
  2. Let them cook for 6 hours
  3. Mash the beans and pork skin until fully mashed
  4. Heat up tortillas on a pan
  5. Combine torillas with beans and shredded cheese
  6. Done
